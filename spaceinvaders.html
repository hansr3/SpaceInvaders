How To Play:

Destroy all the aliens to win the game
Move the ship right and left as shown by the controls above to avoid the alien bullets
Your ship will be destroyed if you got hit by one of the alien bullets
You can destroy the aliens by shooting them and gain points
Your score is indicated at the upper left screen of the game
To shoot you can use the Space key as shown by the controlls
When combining the arrow and Space key, your bullets will travel diagonally, right or left depending on which arrow are you pressing
Each alien has different scores and they shoot at different rates randomly

Octopus aliens (purple circle) are wortth 10 points each
Crab aliens (red circle) are wortth 20 points each
Squid aliens (white circle) are wortth 30 points each

If any alien reached passed the tip of your ship (i.e. bottom)
the game will end and you loose
